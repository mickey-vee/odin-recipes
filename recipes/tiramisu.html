Tiramisu

Description: Classic Tiramisu

This creamy classic Tiramisu recipe is rich in coffee flavor and layered with mascarpone, ladyfingers, and cocoa powder. It's been one of the most beloved Italian desserts around the world, and its smooth texture makes it a favorite among dessert lovers.

Ingredients

  1. Egg yolks
  2. Sugar
  3. Mascarpone cheese
  4. Heavy whipping cream
  5. Strong brewed coffee
  6. Ladyfingers
  7. Cocoa powder
  8. Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar together until smooth.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the mixture and beat until creamy.
  3. Whip the heavy cream and fold into the mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers into coffee and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spread the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat the layers and finish with a dusting of cocoa powder.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving.

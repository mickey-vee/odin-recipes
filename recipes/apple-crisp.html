Oatmeal Cookie Apple Crisp

Description: Oatmeal Cookie Apple Crisp

This delicious apple crisp combines the sweetness of baked apples with a crispy oatmeal cookie topping. It's perfect for fall and a delightful twist on a classic apple dessert. Serve it warm with a scoop of ice cream for a cozy treat.

Ingredients

  1. Apples (peeled and sliced)
  2. Sugar
  3. Cinnamon
  4. Oats
  5. Flour
  6. Butter
  7. Brown sugar
  8. Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare a baking dish.
  2. Mix apples with sugar and cinnamon, then layer them in the dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine oats, flour, brown sugar, and butter to make the crisp topping.
  4. Spread the topping evenly over the apples.
  5. Bake until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.
  6. Let cool slightly and serve warm with ice cream.
